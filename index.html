FAQ

Questions, answered

Honest answers while we’re still early.

Do I need to add other people?

No. You can use Stack solo and still get full goal tracking and progress views.

Who can see my activity?

By default, only you can see your activity and progress updates.

Can I reset and start over?

Yes. You can archive a stack and start fresh anytime.

When are you launching?

We’re inviting waitlist folks in waves. Join the list and we’ll email you when your spot opens.

Where will Stack be available?

We’ll share supported countries and platforms (iOS / Android / web) as we lock scope for v1.
