Privacy & trust

Social doesn’t mean public. Stack is designed for small, intentional groups — not an audience.

Private circles

Only people you invite can see your circle. No open leaderboards by default.

You control visibility

We’re building controls so you choose what’s shared (amounts vs. milestones vs. streaks).

Policies

Full privacy policy and terms will ship before public accounts. Until then, we only collect what the waitlist needs.

FAQ

Honest answers while we’re still early.

Do you hold or move my money?

Not in this first landing chapter. Details on banking, balances, and partners will be spelled out clearly before launch.

What exactly do friends see?

The goal is “enough to stay accountable, not enough to feel exposed.” Expect configurable sharing — we’re not building a broadcast feed.

Can I leave a circle?

Yes. Leaving should be one tap, with clear messaging to your group so it doesn’t feel ghosty.

When are you launching?

We’re inviting waitlist folks in waves. Join the list and we’ll email you when your spot opens.

Where will Stack be available?

We’ll share supported countries and platforms (iOS / Android / web) as we lock scope for v1.

Who’s building Stack?

A small team obsessed with behavior design and honest finance UX. Say hi on LinkedIn.
